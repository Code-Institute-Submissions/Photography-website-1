The Rider

The term “biker” can be overwhelming sometimes, in old days biker was consider to be a criminal member of a gang, of course til this day there are gangs associated with motorcycles. Fortunately now these gangs are not active as before. Over the years opinions and views change witch brings the name “biker” to good side of the story. In my opinion this name represents your life style not in a criminal way but opposite, chase of freedom, excitement and exploration. When meeting people with the same passion and life style it's easy to make friends or even family members. This section was shot with Sony a7riii and Nikon D3. My dear friends, the colourful differences is amazing from Engineers to PHD graduates. I have this saying “ You can’t have intelligence without passion “ .
