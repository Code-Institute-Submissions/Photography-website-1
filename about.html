Hi my name is Gvidas and iv been in love with Motorcycles since I was 15 years old.
I grown up in Lithuania, beck in my days I couldn’t afford a motorcycle so it was just a dream for me. Lucky things changed and I end up here in Copenhagen. My first bike was from 1960s, I had to learn hot to fix if something went wrong, this experience pushed my interest in motorcycle modelling, building a style I visioned was one of my dreams. Don’t get me wrong I do love modern motorcycles as well, in fact that’s what pushed me to start racing professionally.
My admiration of looks and feel of these machines helped me to realise other passion was Photography. Got my first camera and when for ride with my close friends to try out this feeling I got.
It was incredible for me I could capture something in motion and to tell a simple story behind a picture. We all felt felt love but to keep that feeling forever is truly a gift !
